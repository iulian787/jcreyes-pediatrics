Privacy Notice

This page provides a general overview of how we respect your privacy.

JC Reyes Pediatrics is committed to safeguarding patient information and treating all families with respect. We follow standard privacy practices for medical offices and limit access to patient information to authorized team members.

We collect only the information needed to provide care, coordinate appointments, and communicate with families. We do not sell patient information.

Online forms on this website are intended for appointment requests only. Please do not submit protected health information through website forms.

This page is for general informational purposes and is not a substitute for a full Notice of Privacy Practices. Please contact our office for additional details.
