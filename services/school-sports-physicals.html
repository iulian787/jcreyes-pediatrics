School & Sports Physicals

Clearances and forms so your child is ready for school, camp, and sports.

We provide school, camp, and sports physicals to keep children active and safe. These visits focus on health history, growth, and any concerns that could affect participation.

Families should bring required forms to the visit. We complete documentation and review any needed follow-ups to help your child participate confidently.

We also answer questions about nutrition, injury prevention, and overall wellness for active kids.

What to Expect

  • Review of health history and current concerns.
  • Completed forms for school, camp, or sports programs.
  • Guidance on healthy activity and injury prevention.

When to Call

Schedule a physical before school deadlines or sports tryouts to avoid last-minute stress.
