Immunizations

Protective care that supports both your child and the community.

Immunizations are an important part of preventive care. Our team reviews vaccine schedules, answers questions, and keeps records up to date to support school and travel needs.

We focus on keeping families informed with clear guidance and practical tips for what to expect after vaccines, including comfort strategies.

If your child is behind on vaccines, we can create a personalized catch-up plan.

What to Expect

  • Review of vaccine history and upcoming recommendations.
  • Time to discuss concerns and plan next steps.
  • Documentation for school and childcare forms.

When to Call

Contact us if you need vaccine records, are preparing for school enrollment, or have questions about immunization timing.
