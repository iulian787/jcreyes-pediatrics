About JC Reyes Pediatrics

We are dedicated to supporting healthy growth with attentive, family-centered pediatric care.

Meet the Team

Our physicians partner with families to create a calm, supportive experience at every visit.

Dr. Joselito Reyes, MD

Dr. Reyes brings a calm, reassuring presence to every visit and works closely with parents to create a personalized care plan for each child.

Dr. Ligia Grindeanu, MD

Dr. Ligia Grindeanu focuses on preventive care, patient education, and helping families feel confident between checkups.

Care Team

Our clinical and office staff coordinate every step so families feel supported from check-in to follow-up.

Our Approach

Our care is built on open communication and respectful partnership. We encourage questions, share clear next steps, and focus on what helps your child feel safe and supported.

Families can expect thoughtful guidance on growth, nutrition, sleep, development, and school readiness. We work with you to make each visit productive and stress-free.
